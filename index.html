Game Rules

Initial Setup

Tap PLAY and aim your camera at the board. Wait for the graphics to appear.

Tap the Red 3D Dice floating above the board to open the dice page and Start the Game!

1-2 Players: Use the SWITCH PLAYER button after each roll.

Board Tracking

The Orange Square shows your current position. Watch your device to see where you land.

Special Spaces
🍟🍔

Landing on NEGATIVE SPACES moves you BACKWARDS.

🥬🍇

Landing on POSITIVE SPACES moves you FORWARD.

🌀

PORTALS automatically teleport you across the board.

🧠

CHALLENGE SPOTS open a Quiz. Answer correctly for a BONUS MOVE!
