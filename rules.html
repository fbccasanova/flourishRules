Game Rules

Initial Setup

This is a 1-2 Player Game.

Tap PLAY, aim your device at the board, and wait for the graphics to VIRTUALLY APPEAR.

Gameplay Loop

Tap the Dice on your screen to begin your turn.

2-Player Mode: You must manually tap SWITCH PLAYER after each roll to hand over the turn.

Only move the amount shown on the dice. Watch your device's tracking to see exactly where you land.

Board Mechanics
🍔🍟

Landing on a NEGATIVE SPACE moves you BACKWARDS.

🥗🍇

Landing on a POSITIVE SPACE moves you FORWARD.

🌀

Landing on a PORTAL teleports you across the board automatically.

❓

Landing on CHALLENGE SPOTS opens a Quiz. Get it right for a BONUS MOVE!

Pro-Tip: You can drag the dice screen higher or lower to get a better view of the physical board while playing.
